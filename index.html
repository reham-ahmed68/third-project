Popular destenation in Egypt

GIZA CITY
Major tourist destenation include millenia-old monuments in the nile valley. principle among them are the pyramidsand great sphinx at Giza
LUXOR CITY
Loxur, about 500km south of cairo ,it is the ancient city of Thebes. it includes the ruins of the temple complexes at karnak and luxor, which stand within the modern city. in the opposite side of the nile river lie the monuments, temples and tomps of the West Bank Necroplis, which include the valley of the king and the valley of the Queens.
SINAI PENINSULA
sinai has the beach resorts of shatrm el shaikh, Dahab,Nuweiba and Taba as well as locations mentioned in the Bible such as Mount sinai ("Jabal Musa") saint catherine's Monastery may be the oldest working christian monastery in the world
